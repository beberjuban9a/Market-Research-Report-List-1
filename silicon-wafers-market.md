<p><ul><li>N-type</li><li>P-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon wafer market is primarily divided into N-type and P-type wafers, each with distinct electrical properties. N-type wafers are doped with elements like phosphorus, providing extra electrons that enhance conductivity, making them suitable for high-efficiency solar cells and advanced electronic devices. Conversely, P-type wafers are doped with materials like boron, creating "holes" in the silicon structure that facilitate charge flow. Both types are critical in the semiconductor industry, with applications spanning microelectronics to renewable energy technologies.</p></p>
