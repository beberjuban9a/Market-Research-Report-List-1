<p><p>The angiography machine market includes various imaging modalities for visualizing blood vessels. X-ray angiography uses X-rays to capture detailed images of arteries and veins. CT angiography employs computed tomography to produce cross-sectional images, offering enhanced visualization of vascular structures. Magnetic resonance angiography utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves for 3D imaging of blood vessels without radiation. MR-CT angiography combines features of both MRI and CT to leverage their strengths, providing comprehensive vascular assessments with high accuracy.</p></p>
