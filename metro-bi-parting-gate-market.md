<p><p>Metro bi-parting gates come in two main market types: single movement and double movement. Single movement gates open by sliding in one direction, allowing quick access while maintaining space efficiency. In contrast, double movement gates consist of two panels that slide apart from the center, offering a wider entrance while enhancing aesthetic appeal. Both designs provide safety and convenience for passengers in metro stations, facilitating smooth crowd management and improving overall transit experiences.</p></p>
