<p><ul><li>Horizontal Closed Tank</li><li>Silo and Vertical Closed Tank</li><li>Open Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The milk tank market is categorized into several types based on design and functionality. Horizontal closed tanks are cylindrical containers ideal for bulk storage, efficiently managing space. Silo tanks are tall and conical, providing high capacity and easy access for loading. Vertical closed tanks feature a vertical structure, optimizing storage in limited spaces while maintaining milk quality. Open tanks, often used in processing, allow for easy access but may expose milk to contamination risks. Each type serves distinct storage and processing needs in the dairy industry.</p></p>
