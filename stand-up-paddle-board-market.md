Solid SUP boards are typically made from foam, fiberglass, or epoxy, offering durability and performance for flat water and surf conditions. They are generally preferred by experienced paddlers seeking stability and speed. In contrast, inflatable SUP boards are lightweight and portable, making them ideal for recreational users and travelers. They provide convenience, easy storage, and are less prone to damage, appealing to a broader audience.</p></p>
