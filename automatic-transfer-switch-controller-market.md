<p><p>The Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) Controller market consists of two primary types: panel-mounted and DIN rail mounted options. Panel-mounted controllers are integrated directly onto electrical panels, offering a consolidated and user-friendly interface, often used in commercial and industrial settings. Conversely, DIN rail mounted controllers are designed for modular installation on standard DIN rails, providing flexibility and ease of integration in varied applications, particularly in control cabinets. Both types ensure reliable power transfer and enhanced management of electrical systems.</p></p>
