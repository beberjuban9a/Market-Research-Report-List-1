<p><p>The vapor traps market consists primarily of two types: activated carbon vapor traps and cold vapor traps. Activated carbon vapor traps employ activated carbon to adsorb volatile organic compounds and odors from gases, effectively purifying the air. Conversely, cold vapor traps use low temperatures to condense and capture vapors, typically for applications requiring the extraction of specific substances without chemical reactions. Both types serve critical roles in environmental protection and industrial processes, ensuring cleaner emissions and enhanced air quality.</p></p>
