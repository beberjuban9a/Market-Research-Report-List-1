<p><p>The electromagnetic pump market for liquid metals primarily comprises two types: conduction pumps and induction pumps. Conduction pumps utilize the Lorentz force to drive liquid metal through a conductive channel, often favored for their efficiency and direct control over flow rates. In contrast, induction pumps employ electromagnetic fields to induce flow in conductive liquids, benefiting applications where mechanical components are less desirable. Both types are essential in industries like nuclear, aerospace, and material processing, facilitating efficient liquid metal handling.</p></p>
