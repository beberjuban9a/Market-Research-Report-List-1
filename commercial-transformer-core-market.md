<p><strong>The Commercial Transformer Core Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steel Laminated Cores</li><li>Solid Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial transformer core market primarily consists of two types: steel laminated cores and solid cores. Steel laminated cores, made from thin sheets of electrical steel, minimize energy losses due to eddy currents and enhance efficiency, making them suitable for high-performance transformers. Solid cores, on the other hand, are crafted from a single piece of magnetic material, providing robust construction but potentially higher energy losses. Both types are essential for transforming electrical energy in various applications, each serving specific efficiency and design needs.</p></p>
