<p><p>The EPDM rubber compound market features two primary types of polymerization: suspension polymerization and solution polymerization. Suspension polymerization involves dispersing the monomer droplets in a continuous phase, leading to particles that have a high molecular weight and improved processing characteristics. In contrast, solution polymerization occurs in a solvent, resulting in a more uniform product with enhanced control over molecular weight and copolymer composition. Both methods contribute to varying properties and applications of EPDM rubber, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
