<p><p>The tractor tire market comprises three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing excellent traction and shock absorption, ideal for various terrains. Solid tires are constructed from rubber and do not require inflation, offering durability and puncture resistance, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Polyurethane tires combine flexibility and resilience, often used in specialized settings where low weight and high performance are essential. Together, these tire types cater to diverse agricultural and industrial needs.</p></p>
