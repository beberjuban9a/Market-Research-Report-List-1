<p><p>The snake venom antiserum market is categorized into two main types: polyvalent and monovalent antiserum. Polyvalent antiserum is designed to neutralize venoms from multiple snake species, making it versatile for treating bites from various snakes. In contrast, monovalent antiserum targets a specific species, offering a focused and potent treatment for particular snake bites. The choice between these options depends on factors like the geographic region, prevalence of snake species, and clinical requirements for effective treatment.</p></p>
