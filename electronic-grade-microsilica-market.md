<p><p>The Electronic Grade Microsilica market is divided into two main types: angular and spherical microsilica. Angular microsilica features sharp edges and is commonly used in applications requiring higher surface area and robust mechanical properties. It offers enhanced bonding and strength in composite materials. In contrast, spherical microsilica has smooth, rounded particles that improve flowability and evenly distribute within mixtures, making it ideal for electronics and high-performance concrete applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on physical properties.</p></p>
