<p><ul><li>Three-Piece Cans</li><li>Two-Piece Cans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recyclable aluminum beverage can market is primarily divided into two types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are constructed from three separate parts—top, body, and bottom—typically welded together, offering durability and versatility. In contrast, two-piece cans are made from a single aluminum sheet, formed into a cylindrical shape with a seamless design, enhancing structural integrity and reducing material usage. Both types are popular for beverages due to their lightweight nature, recyclability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
