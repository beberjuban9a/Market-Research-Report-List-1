<p><p>The hollow fiber filter market is categorized into microfiltration and ultrafiltration types, each serving distinct applications in water treatment and industrial processes. Microfiltration hollow fiber filters are designed to remove larger particles, bacteria, and sediments, functioning at lower pressure. In contrast, ultrafiltration hollow fiber filters target smaller impurities, including viruses and high molecular weight compounds, operating at higher pressures. Both filtration methods enhance water purity and clarity, with specific uses in pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental sectors.</p></p>
