<p><strong>The IR (Infrared) Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared Detectors (SWIR)</li><li>Mid Wave Infrared Detectors (MWIR)</li><li>Long Wave Infrared Detectors (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared (IR) detector market is segmented into three main types based on wavelength sensitivity: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) detectors. SWIR detectors operate between 0.9 to 2.5 micrometers, suitable for applications like spectroscopy and imaging. MWIR detectors, covering 3 to 5 micrometers, are ideal for thermal imaging and industrial processes. LWIR detectors, ranging from 8 to 14 micrometers, are predominantly used in security, surveillance, and temperature measurement. Each type serves specific applications, driving diverse market demand.</p></p>
