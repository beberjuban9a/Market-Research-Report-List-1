<p><p>The denture base resin market consists of three main types: heat cure acrylic resin, self-curing acrylic resin, and cold cure acrylic resin. Heat cure acrylic resin is processed using high temperatures, resulting in durable and heat-resistant dentures. Self-curing acrylic resin hardens at room temperature, offering convenience for quick repairs and fabrications. Cold cure acrylic resin, also known as chemically activated resin, hardens without heat, making it suitable for immediate dentures. Each type has distinct applications, catering to various clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
