<p><p>PTFE venting solutions in the packaging market primarily include adhesive vents and vents without backing material. Adhesive vents are designed to adhere to packaging surfaces, allowing gases to escape while maintaining a barrier against contaminants. In contrast, vents without backing material offer flexibility and can be directly integrated into packaging designs, facilitating venting without additional adhesives. Both types enhance product integrity by preventing pressure build-up, thereby ensuring optimal preservation of contents through effective gas exchange.</p></p>
