<p><p>The Automatic Milking Machines market consists of two main types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic milking machines. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention for milking, making them suitable for small to medium-sized farms seeking efficiency while retaining a hands-on approach. In contrast, fully-automatic milking machines operate independently, allowing for continuous, labor-saving milking without human involvement. This makes them ideal for larger dairy operations focused on maximizing productivity and reducing labor costs, catering to the evolving needs of the dairy industry.</p></p>
