<p><p>The Positive Material Identification (PMI) market primarily encompasses two key technologies: X-ray Fluorescence (XRF) and Optical Emission Spectroscopy (OES). XRF utilizes high-energy X-rays to excite atoms in a sample, allowing for rapid non-destructive elemental analysis. OES, on the other hand, involves ionizing a sample to emit light, which is then analyzed for compositional information. Both methods are essential for verifying material composition in industries like manufacturing and construction, ensuring compliance with safety and quality standards.</p></p>
