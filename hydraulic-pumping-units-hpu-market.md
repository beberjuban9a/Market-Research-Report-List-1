<p><p>Hydraulic Pumping Units (HPUs) are critical for oil extraction, utilizing hydraulic power to operate pumps. The two primary market types are Beam Pumping Units and Beamless Pumping Units. Beam Pumping Units, commonly known as rod pumps, are characterized by their distinct beam structure that transfers power to lift fluids from the well. In contrast, Beamless Pumping Units employ a more compact design without a beam, offering quieter operation and reduced maintenance while maintaining efficient fluid extraction capabilities.</p></p>
