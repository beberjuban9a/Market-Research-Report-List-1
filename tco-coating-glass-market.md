<p><p>The TCO (Transparent Conductive Oxide) coating glass market comprises three primary types: ITO (Indium Tin Oxide), FTO (Fluorine Doped Tin Oxide), and AZO (Aluminum Doped Zinc Oxide). ITO is widely used in displays and solar cells for its excellent conductivity and transparency. FTO is favored for high-temperature applications and is often used in solar panels and sensors. AZO offers a cost-effective alternative with good conductivity and is increasingly used in various optoelectronic applications, including touch screens and lighting.</p></p>
