<p><p>The semiconductor Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) materials market consists primarily of CMP pads and CMP slurries. CMP pads are abrasive materials used to support the polishing process, ensuring uniform planarization of semiconductor wafers. CMP slurries, on the other hand, are liquid mixtures containing abrasive particles and chemical agents that facilitate the removal of material from the wafer surface. Both components are essential for achieving the desired surface finish and flatness in semiconductor manufacturing, critical for device performance.</p></p>
