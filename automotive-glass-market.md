<p><ul><li>Laminated Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li><li>Special Function Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive glass market encompasses three primary types: laminated glass, tempered glass, and special function glass. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers bonded together, enhancing safety by preventing shattering during impacts. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and shatter resistance, commonly used in sidelights and rear windows. Special function glass includes features like tinted, heated, or sound-insulating glass, providing added comfort and performance. Each type plays a crucial role in vehicle safety, aesthetics, and functionality.</p></p>
