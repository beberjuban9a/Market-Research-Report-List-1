<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Single-mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber cable market primarily comprises two types: multimode fiber and single-mode fiber. Multimode fiber is designed for short-distance communication, utilizing multiple light paths to transmit data, making it ideal for local area networks and data centers. In contrast, single-mode fiber allows only one light path, enabling long-distance transmission with higher bandwidth and reduced signal attenuation, making it suitable for telecommunication networks and long-range data communication. Both types serve distinct applications based on distance and data requirements.</p></p>
