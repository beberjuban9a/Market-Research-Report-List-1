<p><p>The high-efficiency solar modules market primarily features two advanced technologies: Heterojunction (HJT) cells and Interdigitated Back Contact (IBC) cells. HJT cells combine crystalline silicon with thin film layers, enhancing efficiency and performance, particularly in low light conditions. IBC cells, on the other hand, have contacts on the rear side, allowing for better light absorption and reduced shading losses. Both technologies contribute to improved energy output and are pivotal in driving innovation in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
