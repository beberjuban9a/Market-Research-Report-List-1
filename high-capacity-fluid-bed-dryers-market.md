<p><ul><li>Static Fluid-bed Dryer</li><li>Vibrating Fluid-bed Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-capacity fluid-bed dryer market consists of two primary types: static fluid-bed dryers and vibrating fluid-bed dryers. Static fluid-bed dryers utilize a fixed bed of material through which heated air is passed, promoting efficient drying by maximizing surface area exposure. In contrast, vibrating fluid-bed dryers incorporate vibration to enhance particle movement, improving uniformity and drying efficiency. Both types are widely used in industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals, offering effective solutions for bulk material drying.</p></p>
