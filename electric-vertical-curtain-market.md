<p><ul><li>Infrared Remote Control</li><li>Radio Remote Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vertical Curtain Market can be categorized into two primary types based on control mechanisms: Infrared Remote Control and Radio Remote Control. Infrared Remote Control systems utilize infrared signals to operate the curtains, requiring a direct line of sight between the remote and the curtain motor. In contrast, Radio Remote Control systems use radio frequencies, allowing for operation without direct sight, enabling greater flexibility and convenience in controlling the curtains from different positions within a space.</p></p>
