<p><strong>The Well Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Downhole Equipment</li><li>Surface Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The well testing equipment market is divided into two main types: downhole equipment and surface equipment. Downhole equipment includes tools and instruments used within the wellbore, such as pressure gauges, flowmeters, and sample collection devices, essential for evaluating reservoir properties and fluid dynamics. Surface equipment encompasses the tools and systems used above ground, including separators, flare stacks, and data acquisition systems, which facilitate the measurement and processing of produced fluids and ensure safe and efficient operations during testing.</p></p>
