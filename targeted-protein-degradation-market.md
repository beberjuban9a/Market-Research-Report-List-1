<p><p>Targeted Protein Degradation (TPD) is an innovative therapeutic approach that enhances the body's natural process of eliminating unwanted proteins by harnessing the cell's ubiquitin-proteasome system. This method allows for precise modulation of protein levels, offering a powerful strategy for treating various diseases, including cancer and neurodegenerative disorders. TPD utilizes bifunctional molecules, such as PROTACs (proteolysis-targeting chimeras), that guide the ubiquitination of target proteins, leading to their degradation.</p><p>The Targeted Protein Degradation Market is experiencing substantial growth, driven by increasing investments in drug development, a rise in chronic disease prevalence, and advancements in medicinal chemistry. Furthermore, the integration of artificial intelligence in drug discovery is expected to accelerate the identification of suitable TPD targets, enhancing the efficiency of the development pipeline. Overall, TPD represents a promising area poised for significant advancements in the coming years.</p></p>
