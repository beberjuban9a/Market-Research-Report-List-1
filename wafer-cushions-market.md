<p><p>The wafer cushions market comprises two main types: conductive polyethylene cushions and anti-static polyurethane cushions. Conductive polyethylene cushions are designed to dissipate static electricity, ensuring safe handling and protection of sensitive electronic components. In contrast, anti-static polyurethane cushions provide cushioning while preventing static charge build-up, making them ideal for packaging delicate items. Both types play a crucial role in safeguarding products during shipping and storage, minimizing damage and maintaining product integrity in electronic and sensitive applications.</p></p>
